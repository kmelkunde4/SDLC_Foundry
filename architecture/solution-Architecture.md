\# SDLC Foundry - Solution Architecture



\## Overview



SDLC Foundry is an AI-powered multi-agent platform that accelerates the RPA Software Development Lifecycle.



The solution transforms business requirements into automation-ready outputs using a combination of AI agents, human approvals, and UiPath orchestration.
